Masha Bucher

I fund startups and tell their stories.

In 2018, I founded Day One Ventures, an early-stage venture capital firm that pioneered a new model in VC by spearheading communications for our portfolio companies.

At the core of my professional ethos is a deep-seated belief in the transformative power of art, culture, and storytelling in technology and business. By harnessing these, we can bridge the widening gap between society and technology.

At Day One Ventures, we not only fund startups but play an active role in helping our founders win by shaping their narratives and telling their stories to the world, fostering their growth, and increasing their market impact.
‍
Our portfolio is a testament to this success; in five years since launching, I've backed 8 companies that reached unicorn status (over $1B value), our portfolio captured over $35B in value successfully navigated 20 exits to industry giants such as Twitter, Doordash, and MasterCard.
‍
Before founding Day One, I honed my skills as an angel investor and founder of a PR studio, collaborating with companies like Houzz, HotelTonight, Gett, Toptal, Pandadoc and more. Earlier in my career, I was the Vice President of Communications at the multi-billion-dollar data protection company Acronis, and prior to that the Head of Communications at the venture capital firm Runa Capital.
